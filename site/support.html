Support

Quick fixes for the common snags, and where to reach us when they aren't enough.

Common snags

Pairing sits on "Connecting..." and times out

Bark and your signer must share at least one relay. Check the relay list in your bunker URI matches one your signer actually connects to, and that the signer is unlocked and online.

If the signer needs you to approve new clients, approve Bark there (Heartwood shows a button prompt; hosted bunkers show a queue). Then hit Retry in the popup.

A website says window.nostr is missing

If privacy mode is on, Bark is hidden from every site without a site rule. Open the popup, expand Signing Policies, and add the site (or untick privacy mode), then reload the page.

Bark also only injects on https pages (plus localhost), so http-only sites won't see it.

Signing works, then stalls after the browser sits idle

The first request after a long idle can take a few seconds while the service worker wakes and reconnects to the relay; Bark retries this automatically. If it hangs beyond that, open the popup and run the sign Test, which reports exactly where the chain is stuck.

The approval window never appears

Check the toolbar badge: queued approvals show a count, and clicking the Bark icon surfaces the queue. Some window managers open the approval behind the browser; it's a small 420px window titled Bark.

I want to move to a new signer

Add the new signer with "+ Add Signer" (URI or QR), confirm signing works with the Test button, then remove the old instance. Site policies are kept; they belong to Bark, not the signer.

Reach us

Bugs and feature requests

GitHub issues is the fastest route. A good report includes your browser, signer type, and what the popup's sign Test says.

Security issues

Never post vulnerabilities publicly. The security policy explains how to reach the maintainer privately.

Security policy

One thing we will never ask you for: your nsec, your seed phrase, or your bunker URI with its secret. Anyone who does is not us.
